Guide to Google Web Fonts

1First, you would want to look at the fonts at http://www.google.com/webfonts.

2See something that you really like? Copy that specific font name that you desire.

3Go back to your website's back-end. Quickly click on the icon . A popup dialog will come up and it's time to paste your favorite font name here.

4Select the suitable variant and subset for your font

5Prefer to custom CSS? Check the Custom CSS box and work on it.

6 Hit the apply button then save. You're good to go.
